Why Neeruku Nandri?

Right from the day we’re born until our last breath, water is our constant companion. Anything that paves way for a harmonious world holds highest importance in the world and needs to be educated to every human being so that it can be followed with discipline. When Guruji Mithreshiva realized the truth about water, he felt the need of the hour was to create the emotional connect between humans and the supreme living being "Water". We wish to turn this vision into reality by making the world understand the connectivity we have with water, thereby connecting people and nature through the Neerukku Nandri revolution. It’s high time that we re-establish our emotional bond with our beloved friend and the only way to reconnect is to be thankful to her for all that she has done in our life.
